Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

The short version

This is a small informational website for Two Desks. It has no accounts, no forms, no newsletter signup, and no advertising trackers.

Contact email

If you email us at hello [at] twodesks [dot] app, we receive your email address and whatever you choose to include in the message. We use that information to reply to you. We do not sell it or add it to a marketing list.

Cloudflare and hosting

The site is published through GitHub Pages with Cloudflare in front of the domain. Cloudflare may process technical data such as IP address, browser details, request metadata, and security signals for routing, caching, abuse prevention, and challenge checks.

The live Cloudflare-proxied site may inject Cloudflare-served scripts for security checks. Those scripts are provided by Cloudflare for site protection; they are not advertising scripts.

Analytics

We do not add a separate analytics script in this website repository. We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking pixels on this site.

Our apps

Each Two Desks app has its own privacy policy. App privacy details are handled on the app's own website or App Store listing.

Questions

Email us at hello [at] twodesks [dot] app.
