About

Two people, two desks, one thoughtful product at a time.

We are Harry and Vasso. The name is literal: we work from two desks at home.

We build mobile apps we would actually want to use. Private by default, and useful without trying to take up your whole day.

What we believe

The product should do its job, then get out of the way.

  • Privacy first Your data should stay yours. We build apps that respect privacy by default.
  • No dark patterns No manipulative streaks, engagement tricks, or artificial urgency.
  • Accessible to all Good tools should be accessible to everyone, not only to people who can afford them.
